# Black-Eye-by-Lau
Water Proof Makeup Remover
Ingredients:
Taurus Tallow, Cocos Nucifera, (Coconut) Oil, Mel (Manuka) Honey, Curcuma Longa Root Extract, Tocopherol, Vanilla Plantofilia Fruit Extract, Cinnamon and Jasmine.

* Application: Apply a small amount to fingertips and gently massage onto dry face, focusing on areas with makeup.
* Removal: Remove with a cotton pad or tissue.
* Avoidance: Avoid direct contact with eyes and broken or irritated skin.
* Reapply as needed.
